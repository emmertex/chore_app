Help / Explanation

  • Earning Points
    • Chores have a set value of points
    • Some chores earn more if completed in the morning
    • Chores are worth 2 to 4 points per minute
    • You can add a chore as a custom chore if not available
    • Every day some bonus points are added for free
  • Losing Points
    • Chores not completed have half their value removed from your points
  • Spending Points
    • Every minute of time spent on screens, such as iPad, TV, Computer and XBox, cost 1 point
    • If over 300 points are available, you can convert 100 points to pocket money.
  • Penalties
    • All chores claimed must be approved, and may incur penalties
    • Penalties may be for incomplete job, or finished a partially completed chore
  • Adjustments, Refunds of points
    • If you watched YouTube on iPad while playing on XBox for an example, ask for a refund
    • If you left a game running by accident, ask for a refund
    • If the game was educational, ask for a refund
